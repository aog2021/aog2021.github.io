Work From Home Agreement

The Work Health and Safety (WHS) checklist is designed to help employees and managers to assess the WHS risks at home, privately-funded or satellite office. The checklist should be read together with the ‘Work from home’ guideline.

Guidelines are as follows:

  • Understanding of the WORK HEALTH AND SAFETY CHECKLIST.
  • Availability of alternative internet connection option.
  • Company issued units are to be used ONLY.

Employees who will be approved of telecommute will be required of the following:

  • STRICT Time IN and OUT of Hurey and/or Casino (following the normal assigned working schedule). ​NO GRACE PERIOD.
  • Be online and responsive on all Work Related platforms during the working hours (Google chat, Email etc.).
  • Be professional.

WFH form is to be COMPLETED and submitted to your Line Manager, along with a photo of your work station and a screenshot of your Internet Speed (Ookla). See sample below.
Failure to comply will result to disapproval of request.

NOTE:
The Management may terminate this agreement if there is an inability to deliver work due to technology limitations or other man-made degradation of service.
